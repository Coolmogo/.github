# Coolmogo

> *Explore new possibilities. Find elegant solutions.*

Welcome to the official GitHub profile for **Coolmogo** — a software consulting and engineering practice based in Sydney, Australia, founded by [Simon Howard](https://www.linkedin.com/in/simon-howard-dn).

We help businesses automate their processes, integrate their systems, and grow with new technologies. From strategy and consulting through to hands-on coding and creative digital projects, Coolmogo brings curiosity, craft, and a relationship-first approach to every engagement.

---

## What We Do

### 🤝 Consulting
We partner with businesses to win back time and eliminate double-handling through software that works *for* them — not the other way around.

- **Business Automation** — Remove manual work and streamline operations
- **System Integrations** — Connect your platforms (Xero, MYOB, and more) so your data stays in sync
- **AI Adoption** — Find practical ways AI can work for your business
- **Business Intelligence** — Visual dashboards and reports to better understand your data
- **Forms & Workflows** — Capture data and control the flow of information across your team
- **Tech Problem Solving** — Unstick hard problems and help you get more from your software

### 💻 Coding
Software engineering from an experienced, passionate developer who loves finding efficient, elegant solutions.

- **Flutter Apps** — Cross-platform apps deployed to iOS, Android, and the web
- **Platform Development** — Lead and senior engineering roles on commercial SaaS products
- **Low-Code & Custom Integrations** — Rapid delivery using modern frameworks

### 🎨 Creative
Where it all started. Coolmogo was born from a love of creativity — video, animation, and games. That spirit of experimentation and playfulness still runs through everything we do.

- **Audio-Visual Experiences** — Immersive, interactive AV projects for events and music festivals
- **Creative Tech** — Exploring the intersection of software and artistic expression
